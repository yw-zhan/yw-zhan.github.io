Biography

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Tsinghua University (2024.7–), working with Prof. Wenwu Zhu and Prof. Xin Wang at the Department of Computer Science and Technology. Before that, I obtained my Ph.D. from Shandong University (2019–2024), supervised by Prof. Xin-Shun Xu. During my Ph.D., I was an intern at the National University of Singapore (2023–2024), working with Prof. Mohan Kankanhalli at the N-CRiPT Research Center. My research interests include Embodied AI, World Models, and Multimodal Understanding
